# Daily Crypto Brief

Read `out/brief-input.md` and produce a concise but high-signal daily brief.

Output file:

`out/daily-brief-YYYY-MM-DD.md`

Structure:
1. Overnight state
2. Broad crypto news
3. BTC
4. ETH
5. SOL
6. TA / analyst updates
7. Consensus vs disagreement
8. What matters next
9. Source appendix

Rules:
- separate factual news from technical-analysis opinion
- explicitly flag weakly sourced claims
- preserve source URLs in the appendix
- do not invent prices, catalysts, or on-chain metrics not present in the source bundle
